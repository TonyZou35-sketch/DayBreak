FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers

How does Daybreak integrate with existing systems? -
Daybreak connects via API to your existing ERP, WMS, and planning tools. Our integration layer is designed for enterprise environments, typically going live within 6–8 weeks without disrupting current workflows.
How long until teams see impact? +
Most teams begin seeing measurable reductions in manual planning work within the first 30 days. Forecast accuracy improvements and labor savings typically become visible at full deployment, around week 8–12.
How is AI explainability handled? +
Every decision Dawn makes includes a rationale — the factors weighed, thresholds applied, and confidence level. Planners can inspect any recommendation and understand exactly why it was made before acting on it.
Who uses Daybreak day to day? +
Supply chain planners, demand managers, and operations leads are Dawn's primary users. Leadership and finance teams leverage the dashboards for visibility into decision quality and labor reallocation.
